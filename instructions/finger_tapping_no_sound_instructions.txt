Finger Tapping Task
In this task you will be asked to tap your index finger on either your right or left hand.
While tapping your finger in this way, try match the rate at which your finger is tapping by listening to the beeping sounds played to you in your headphones
There will be a countdown timer in the bottom left corner of the screen which will tell you how much time is left before moving on to the next task.
Once you finish the task, you will be given a period of rest before beginning the next task.
Please ask the experimenter if you have any questions at this time.
